As a Software Engineering Intern at Emerald Energy, I was responsible for the development of the Retrieval Augmented Generation Chatbot- Emerald Energy's HVAC Copilot. Utilizing OpenAI, I created this chatbot with the intent to instruct deployed HVAC technicians on how to install specific hardware such as heatpumps and air conditioners so that they would never have to scour through 200+ pages of instruction manuals. In a UI that is not too different from mobile texting, we would engage the technicians by asking them clarifying questions which would allow us to go through a decision tree to discover any problems.
