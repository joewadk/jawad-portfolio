Extracurriculars

B.Y.T.E @CCNY

I am the Cofounder of B.Y.T.E. @CCNY alongside Fahad Faruqi, Baljinder Hothi, Ishmam Fardin, and Muhammad Khan. The goal of our club is to level the playing field for my fellow CCNY students by giving them the chance to build impressive and well-built projects to show off on their resume. Throughout the semester, each of us will guide two groups of 4 and help them along their journey through app development for their topic of choice. At the end of the semester we will host a Demo Night, where we invite all of our members to visit and see the hardwork and effort our students put into their projects.

Visit byteccny.com to check out all of the cool projects!
